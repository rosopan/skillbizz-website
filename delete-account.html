Delete Your SkillBizz Account

At SkillBizz, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal information. If you would like to permanently delete your account and associated data, you can request account deletion using the method below.

How to Request Account Deletion

1. Log in to the SkillBizz app.

2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account

OR

Send an email to:

support@skillbizz.app

Subject: Delete My SkillBizz Account

Please include the email address registered with your SkillBizz account.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information
  • Profile details
  • Business or Service profile
  • Posts and uploaded images
  • Reviews and ratings
  • Saved preferences
  • Authentication information stored in SkillBizz

Data That May Be Retained

  • Information required for legal compliance.
  • Records required for fraud prevention or dispute resolution.
  • Anonymous analytics that cannot identify you.

Deletion Timeline

Verified deletion requests are generally processed within 7 business days. Once your account is deleted, the action is permanent and cannot be reversed.

Important:
Deleting your account permanently removes access to your SkillBizz profile and all associated information. This process cannot be undone.
